## English

### App Name (30 chars)
BNM - New Muslim Mentoring

### Short Description (80 chars)
Mentoring app: Experienced Muslims guide new Muslims on their journey in Islam.

### Full Description (max. 4000 chars)
BNM (Betreuung neuer Muslime / New Muslim Care) is a mentoring platform that connects new Muslims with experienced mentors. The app provides a structured support program for people who have embraced Islam.

**For Mentees (new Muslims):**
- Personal mentor who accompanies you on your journey
- Structured learning plan with progress tracking
- Gamification elements to keep you motivated
- Direct chat with your mentor
- Questions & Answers section

**For Mentors:**
- Clear dashboard with all mentees
- Track your mentees' progress
- Chat function for personal guidance
- Session documentation

**For Admins & Office:**
- Management of all mentors and mentees
- Automatic gender separation
- Assignment system for mentor-mentee pairs
- Reporting and statistics
- Email notifications

BNM is operated by iman.ngo and supports communities in providing professional and warm care for new Muslims.

**Privacy:** Your data is securely stored in Europe. We do not share personal data with third parties.
